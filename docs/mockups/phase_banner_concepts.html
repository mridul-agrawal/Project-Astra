Project Astra · Art Direction · R01 · Phase Banner

The Turning of the Wheel

Three ways to announce whose hand guides the battle. Each shown at full 16:9 over a simulated map, in all three faction colours.

I
Floating Sigil
Pure glowing text · no backdrop

No rectangle, no panel — just massive radiant typography burning through the battlefield like a divine decree. Multi-layered text-shadow creates a halo that reads over any terrain. Closest to the Fire Emblem reference, reinterpreted with temple-gold warmth.

II
Temple Scroll
Panelled strip · gold borders & orbs

A semi-transparent banner strip with backdrop blur, gold borders, and corner orbs — the same visual language as the existing battle HUD panels. Most polished and production-consistent; the banner feels like it belongs to the same UI family.

III
Dharma Verse
Minimal rules · editorial restraint

Two thin gold rules frame the phase name; a small yantra diamond sits between the words. Maximum negative space, maximum restraint. The map shows through almost unobstructed — the announcement is a whisper, not a shout.
